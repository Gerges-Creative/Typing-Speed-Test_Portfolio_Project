Welcome to the Typing Speed Test Game!

Challenge your typing skills across different levels, each with a unique list of words tailored to the chosen difficulty. The game is designed to push your limits and improve your typing speed. Here's how it works:

  1. Levels & Difficulty:
    Each level presents a new challenge with a distinct set of words. The current level is , where you have seconds to type each word.
  2. Extra Time for the First Word:
    To get you started, you'll have an additional 3 seconds for the first word. This gives you a moment to familiarize yourself with the word set and prepare for the challenge ahead.
  3. Word Count:
    Each level contains a total of words. Your goal is to type them as quickly and accurately as possible.
  4. Scoring:
    Your last score will be displayed on the right, while the score of your current game will be shown on the left. Can you beat your previous score?

Instructions:

  • Select your desired level from the dropdown menu.
  • Press the "Start" button to begin the game.
  • Type the word displayed as quickly and accurately as possible.
  • Press "Enter" to submit each word and move to the next one.
  • The game ends when you complete all the words or run out of time.

Good luck and have fun improving your typing speed!
